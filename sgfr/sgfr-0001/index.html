Halation Celebration ~ 10th Anniversary Tribute to Snow halation
Halation Celebration is a tribute album to Love Live's Christmas classic "Snow halation", where many of our most talented artists got together to remix Snow halation in many different styles in celebration of its 10th anniversary!
